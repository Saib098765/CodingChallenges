ONLINE STORAGE, RETRIEVAL AND AUTHENTICATION OF HEALTHCARE DOCUMENTS





The promise of blockchain technology in health services combined with application layers built atop it,is to be a mechanism that provides utmost privacy.
Sharing of medical records between participants is very challenging because the data might be revealed or tampered during the operational process.
To address these challenges, a blockchain-based electronic medical records system is the key solution.
